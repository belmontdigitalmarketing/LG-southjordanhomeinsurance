Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

SouthJordanHomeInsurance.com, operated by Unified Insurance Partners, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly through our quote request forms, contact forms, and other interactions with our website:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Home address and zip code
  • Home details: year built, square footage, roof age, construction type
  • Home ownership status (own, buying, refinancing)
  • Property characteristics: plumbing type, electrical panel type, wiring updates
  • Insurance preferences and coverage needs
  • Any additional comments or information you provide

We also automatically collect technical data:

  • IP address and approximate location
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Referring website or source

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Connect you with licensed insurance agents who can provide home insurance quotes
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Compare rates from multiple insurance carriers on your behalf
  • Improve our website, services, and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

3. How We Share Your Information

  • Licensed Insurance Agents: Your information is shared with licensed agents and insurance carriers who can provide the quotes and coverage you've requested.
  • Service Providers: We may share information with third-party service providers who assist in operating our website and processing data (e.g., form processing, analytics).
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law, regulation, or legal process.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes unrelated to your insurance inquiry.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to analyze website traffic, understand user behavior, and improve our services. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may limit certain functionality of our website.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

7. Children's Privacy

SouthJordanHomeInsurance.com is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of our website constitutes acceptance of any modified policy.
